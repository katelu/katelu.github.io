Japanese Food Recipe

< Tamagoyaki 卵焼き >

< Ingredients 2 Servings>

  • ★ 2 Eggs
  • ★ 1 Tsp. of Sugar
  • ★ 1 Tsp. of Soy Sauce
  • ★ 1 Tbsp. of Water

< Directioins >

  1. Mix all of the ★ ingredients into a bowl.
  2. Pour 1 Tbsp. of oil into a flying pan.
  3. Pour in 1/3 of the mixed ★ ingredients into the pan. When the egg started to cook, roll up the egg.
  4. Pour the rest of the mixed ★ ingredients into the flying pan.
  5. Roll up the egg again, and Ready to serve!

< Tips >

  • By using a squared flying pan will be easier, but regular small size flying pan will work just fine!
  • By pouring small amount of mixed ★ ingredients everytime, it will make the roll more prettier!
