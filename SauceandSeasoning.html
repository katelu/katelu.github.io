Sauce and Seasoning

Eat well, Live well

Let's learn about common sauce and seasoning Japanese use when making the food! You will be surprised how much sugar and soy sauce we love to use!

Soy Sauce

Soy Sauce is made with Soy Beans. This is the origin of the name. It is the most common sauce that is used.

Sugar

The sugar commonly used is made out od sugar cane or sugar beet. By using sugar, it makes the food sweet.

Mirin

It is a type of Cooking Wine that Japanese commonly use in cooking. The alcohol is made with rice.

Miso

Miso is made out of rice, soy beans, salt, other ingredients. It is also a common seasoning that is used.

Vinegar

Vinegar can make the cooking sour. It is also commonly used when making Sushi.

Salt

It is common ingredients used throughout the world.

Dashi

It is made out of fish and seaweed, and make it to power form.
