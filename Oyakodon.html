Japanese Food Recipe

< Teriyaki 照り焼き >

< Ingredients 2 Servings>

  • 2 Chicken Breast or Chicken Thigh
  • ★ 1~2 Tsp. of Sugar
  • ★ 4 Tbsp. of Soy Sauce
  • ★ 2 Tbsp. of Cooking White Wine
  • ★ 2 Tbsp. of Mirin

< Directioins >

  1. Mix the ★ ingredients into a bowl. Using a plying pan, cook both side of chicken breast/thigh.
  2. Pour the mixed ★ ingredients into the pan. Let it cook with low heat.
  3. Cut the chicken, and Ready to serve!

< Tips >

  • Sugar can be added more or less based on your preference.
  • The mixed ★ ingredients can be burned easily, so please be careful.
  • Mirin can be replace with cooking white wine.
